### How can I reserve a table
To reserve a table, first of all open a restaurant where you want to book a table, then you will see this container
![pic1](https://i.imgur.com/bIX7qrE.png)<br />
Here you have to fill in the form to continue. After clicking the "Reserve" button you should see this page  
![pic2](https://i.imgur.com/fryjnm8.png)<br />
where you will be prompted to fill in all of these fields. These fields are required for restaurants to contact you.
Upon successful completion, you will be redirected to the home page where you will see this alert message. It means, you have finished your table reservation.
![pic3](https://i.imgur.com/bwU3SVD.png)<br />
### What should I do after completing a reservation
On "My Reservation" page, you can see all of your upcoming reservations. After completing your reservation, you just need to arrive at the restaurant on time and you have to show your reservation ID and that's it. After that, the restaurant staff will seat you at your desired reserved seat.
### How to contact restaurants
On any restaurant page below, you can find a contact form. There you can see the contact information about the restaurant, and you can also write your message directly to the restaurant.
![pic4](https://i.imgur.com/UNrkCev.png)<br />
### How can I find the restaurant I want
On the main page, you can see the blue bar labeled "What are you looking for?" where you can search for a restaurant by cuisine and name.
![pic5](https://i.imgur.com/5Y4F6aV.png)<br />
### What should I do to publish restaurants in a website
If you want to cooperate with us, in footer, there is a "For restaurant owners" field. There you can write you plans, information about you and your business and etc. After submiting this for, your data will be sent to the administrations for consideration of further cooperation.
![pic6](https://i.imgur.com/b0LLLJx.png)<br />
After a while you should receive a letter from the administration. And thus you will see the decisions of the administrations. It can be either accepted
![pic7](https://i.imgur.com/UadqyQD.png)<br />
or rejected
![pic8](https://i.imgur.com/pUckqc9.png)<br />
### How to work with clients 
On the restaurant page, you can see all the reservations made by the website users. And they have to come to the restaurant and show the booking ID, thereby the restaurant verifies the identity of the customer and will seat him in his place.
### How to add restaurants
If you open the Restaurants field in the navigation bar. you will be shown all active restaurants. And there is a small button to add a new restaurant. After clicking this button, you have to provide all the detailed information about the restaurant and you're done!
![pic9](https://i.imgur.com/fnnJgwI.png)<br />
